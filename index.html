About Event

Objectives and Goals

Newcomers often suffer from “imposter syndrome”, the feeling they don't belong because they don't have skills, aren't smart enough etc. They’re wrong, of course, but until they feels like they belong they will not be able to have a fulfilling experience.

As a result of those things we decided to plan such an event with the objective of providing the look and feel of a grand hackathon event and through that open the doors towards the world of competitive programming for our beloved first year brothers and sisters.
